Research

Overview
  • My research focuses on quantitative and data -driven approaches to Supply Chain Management, Operations Management, Business Analytics, and Information Systems. I develop models in optimization, predictive analytics, fuzzy logic, multi -criteria decision maki ng, risk and resilience analytics, and digital transformation to enhance decision quality in complex global logistics and operational environments.
  • I maintain a strong scholarly record with 37 peer -reviewed journal articles, 24 conference papers, a book chapter, multiple funded projects (PI, Co -PI, Investigator), and 1,519 citations (h -index: 19; i10-index: 27).
  • My work integrates computer science, analytics, engineering, and business to produce theory - grounded and practice -oriented decision support solutions informed by Industry 4.0 technologies and AI-enabled analytics.
